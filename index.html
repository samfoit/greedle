How to play

Click the roll button to roll the dice!

If the dice are green they helped score points:

  • 1's are 100 points
  • 5's are 50 points
  • 3 of a kind is 100 * the dice number

If all dice become green you can roll all 6 die again!


Choose to either roll again without the green die or pass for your daily score!
